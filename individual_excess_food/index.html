Individual Leftover Food

If you've got extra food and you're not sure what to do with it, we will ensure it reaches a food bank or an NGO. Our trained Food Warriors will recieve your excess food from your homes!



How it works?

Get in touch with us to discuss the amount and type of food.

We will send a team of trained volunteers at your doorstep to collect the food

The food warriors will send the food to food banks or NGOs where it is needed

In this way you will contribute to the society by donating your excess food and we will make sure it reaches the person in need
